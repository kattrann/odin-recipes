Oyakodon!!

Super easy recipe you can cook in less than 30 minutes

Ingredients:
  • Chicken thighs
  • Sake
  • Onion
  • Seasonings
  • Steamed rice
  • Garnish
  • Shichimi Togarashi
Steps
  1. Prepare the ingredients
  2. Cook the onion in seasoning mixutre
  3. Add the chicken and cook until no longer pink
  4. Drizzle egg mixutre in twice
  5. Garnish with green onions
