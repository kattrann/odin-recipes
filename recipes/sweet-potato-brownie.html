Sweet Potato Brownie!!

A healthy option to enjoy your favorite dessert

Ingredients
  • Sweet potato
  • Cocoa powder
  • Pure vanilla extract
  • Nut butter
  • Flour
  • Sweetner
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips
Instructions:
  1. Preheat your oven to 325 F
  2. Whisk nut butter together with mashed sweet potato and pure vanilla extract
  3. Pour the mixutre into a pan
  4. Bake the brownie in 20 minutes
  5. Let the brownie rest until firm
